Puzzle game with physics and drawing for Android
Your way through the dangerous jungles begins.
Draw everything you want to complete levels and wade through jungles.
Be careful in your adventure, avoid dangerous spikes and circular saws. Sometimes this is not easy, but gravity switches, portals and impulses help you to do that.
Features:
  • Exciting physics;
  • Drawing of physical lines on the screen;
  • Various physical mechanics, such as gravity switches, portals, rotated obstacle, impulses;
  • 64 interesting, unique level;
  • Beautiful music;
The game for all ages. Everyone can find something interesting in this puzzle.
